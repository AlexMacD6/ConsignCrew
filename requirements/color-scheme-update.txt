# Color Scheme Update for TreasureHub Branding

## Overview
Update the entire application's color scheme to use the TreasureHub brand colors consistently throughout the landing page and components.

## Brand Colors
- **Primary Brand Color**: #d4af3d (treasure-500)
- **Secondary Brand Color**: #825e08 (treasure-600) 
- **Accent Color**: #f4d03f (treasure-400)
- **Dark Brand Color**: #5d4a00 (treasure-700)
